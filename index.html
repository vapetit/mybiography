About me

Welcome to one of my most awaited blogs: NEW YORK! I will give a little bit of context for those who don’t know about the program so you can understand more about the idea of this trip.

As most of you know, I came as an au pair to the US in November 2022 and one of the requirements of the program is that you have to do 72hs or 6 credits. I decided to do one of the au pair weekends in New York, which being honest with you, I really recommend doing it.

Photos

On our first night we went to the Harry Potter store, located at 935 Broadway, New York, NY 10010, and after that we went to have dinner at an Argentinian place called “Sabor Argentino” located at 57 7th Ave S, New York, NY 10014. I can’t express in words how much we missed the food from our country and how delicious it was!!!

During our second day we started our tour class. We went to places like Chinatown, Little Italy, and Soho. Also, we took the ferry to Staten Island. Once the day was over we went to another Argentinian place called “Empanadas Soho” located at 180 Mulberry St, New York, NY 10012, to buy some yummy empanadas.

After that we went to see The Vessel, and we went to the Edge, to see one of the most beautiful views from New York! I totally recommend doing at least one of the rooftops. To end the day we went to Times Square to see it at night and back at the hotel.

It’s now our third day in New York and our second day of the tour class, we went to the Brooklyn Bridge, and crossed to Brooklyn. We saw the Manhattan Bridge and the Williamsburg bridge.

After our class we went back to the hotel, changed and went to have dinner at an Italian place called Eataly located at 200 5th Ave, New York, NY 10010, we ate a really good pasta and drank some wine.

Once we finished our dinner we decided to go back to Times Square to take cute pictures and of course… it started raining, which made the pictures and the experience even better! Finally, we went back to the hotel to get some rest for our last day in the city!
